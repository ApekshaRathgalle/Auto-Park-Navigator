Privacy and Policy


At Auto Park Navigator, we are committed to safeguarding the privacy of our users and protecting their personal information. This privacy policy outlines how we collect, use, and disclose the information provided to us through our automated parking system and website. By accessing or using our services, you consent to the practices described in this policy. We take your privacy seriously and strive to ensure that your personal information is handled securely and in accordance with applicable laws and regulations. Please read this privacy policy carefully to understand how we collect, use, and protect your information. If you have any questions or concerns regarding our privacy practices, please don't hesitate to contact us. Thank you for choosing Auto Park Navigator.

Information Collection and Use

Auto Park Navigator collects various types of information from users to provide and improve our automated parking services. This information may include personal data such as name, contact details, vehicle information, and payment details. We collect this information when users register an account, make a reservation, or communicate with our customer support team. Additionally, we may collect usage data, including IP addresses, browser type, device information, and browsing activity, to analyze trends and improve the functionality and security of our services. We use the information collected solely for the purposes of providing and improving our services, processing transactions, communicating with users, and complying with legal obligations. Auto Park Navigator is committed to protecting the confidentiality and security of user information and adheres to industry-standard practices to prevent unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction of personal data.



Data Sharing and Disclosure

Auto Park Navigator may share user information with trusted third-party service providers and business partners to facilitate the provision of our services and enhance user experience. These third parties may include payment processors, hosting providers, marketing agencies, and analytics services. We may also disclose user information in response to legal requests, court orders, or legal processes, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of Auto Park Navigator, our users, or others. Additionally, user information may be shared in connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, in which case users will be notified via email or prominent notice on our website before any change in ownership or control of their personal data. Auto Park Navigator does not sell or rent user information to third parties for marketing purposes without user consent. We take reasonable steps to ensure that any third parties with whom we share user information comply with applicable privacy laws and regulations and provide adequate protection for user data.
